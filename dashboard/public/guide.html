1. Getting Started

1
Create Your Account

Go to Slips page. You'll see a login screen. Pick a username and password (4+ characters). Click SIGN UP.

You start with $1,000 fake bankroll. Your session stays logged in across browser restarts.

2
Pick Your Sport

From the home page, click NBA or UCL to go to that sport's live scores. You can also use the top navigation bar on any page — it shows both sports.

Home→ NBA Scoresor UCL Scores

Explore a Game

Click any game card on the scores page to open the full matchup breakdown with team stats, rosters, player comparisons, and injury reports.

2. Making Betting Slips

1
Go to Slips Page

Click Slips in the nav bar. Toggle between NBA and UCL at the top.

2
Select a Game

Pick an upcoming game from the dropdown. Only scheduled and live games show — finished games are filtered out. Games show the day, time, and spread/total if available.

3
Select a Player

Use the player dropdown to pick a player. Their available betting lines appear below — grouped by stat with the primary line shown and alternate lines in a dropdown.

Click Analysis to see their full statistical breakdown (distributions, Poisson model, trends) without leaving the page.

4
Add Legs to Your Slip

Click Over or Under on any line to add it to your slip. Each button shows:

LineThe threshold (e.g. Over 22.5 points)
OddsAmerican odds from the book (-115, +145, etc.)
Implied %How likely the book thinks this is
BookWhich sportsbook (DraftKings, FanDuel, Model)
5
Set Your Wager & Submit

Your slip preview shows all legs, combined odds, implied probability, and a parlay distribution chart showing P(0 hit), P(1 hit)... P(all hit).

Set your wager amount and click LOCK IT IN.

You can also add props from the Matchup page — click the +O or +U buttons next to any O/U line in the player analysis. A floating slip widget appears in the bottom-right corner. Navigate to Slips to finalize.

4. Understanding Odds & Implied Probability

American Odds

-110Bet $110 to win $100. Implied: 52.4%. Slight favorite.
-200Bet $200 to win $100. Implied: 66.7%. Strong favorite.
+150Bet $100 to win $150. Implied: 40%. Underdog.
+500Bet $100 to win $500. Implied: 16.7%. Big underdog.

Decimal Odds

Click the AM / DEC toggle on the Slips page to switch. Decimal odds = total return per $1 bet. Example: 2.50 means $2.50 back on a $1 bet ($1.50 profit).

Parlay Math

A parlay multiplies the odds of each leg. A 3-leg parlay at -115 each:

Decimal: 1.87 x 1.87 x 1.87 = 6.54x → $100 bet wins $554

Implied probability: 53.5% x 53.5% x 53.5% = 15.3% chance all hit

Go to Analysis page. Three tabs:

7. In-House Bookmaker

1
Create a Line

On the Analysis page, click In-House Book tab. Enter a player name, stat, line (must be a .5 number), and set your Over/Under odds. Set a max wager.

2
Friends Bet Against You

Your line appears in the Open Lines feed. Friends click Over or Under and enter their wager amount. You're the house — if they win, you pay. If they lose, you keep the wager.

8. How Bets Get Resolved

Bets are resolved automatically every 5 minutes after games finish. The system:

1.Fetches box scores from ESPN for all finished NBA + UCL games
2.Matches each slip leg to the correct player's actual stats
3.Compares actual stat vs the line (e.g. player scored 28, line was 22.5 → Over hits)
4.Grades the full slip: all legs must hit for a parlay to win
5.Updates your bankroll and W/L record

What Gets Resolved

You can also click Resolve on the slip feed to trigger manual resolution.
